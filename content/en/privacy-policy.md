### Tracking Technologies and Cookies
So, we use Cookies and other sneaky tracking tech to keep an eye on what's happening on our Service and stash away some info. The tracking tech we're talking about includes beacons, tags, and scripts – all used to gather and track info, helping us level up and analyze our Service. The tech we use might include:

- **Cookies or Browser Cookies**: A Cookie is just a tiny file that gets dropped on your device. You can totally tell your browser to say "no thanks" to all Cookies or to give you a heads-up when one's being sent. But hey, if you don't play along with Cookies, you might miss out on some parts of our Service. Unless you've tweaked your browser settings to reject them, our Service might just be using Cookies.
- **Web Beacons**: Some parts of our Service and even our emails might have these tiny electronic files called web beacons (you might also know them as clear GIFs, pixel tags, or single-pixel GIFs). These bad boys let the Company, for example, count users who've visited those pages or opened an email, and they're used for other cool website stats (like tracking how popular a section is or making sure our systems and servers are all good).

Cookies come in two flavors: "Persistent" or "Session" Cookies. Persistent Cookies stick around on your personal computer or mobile device even when you go offline, while Session Cookies get wiped out the moment you close your web browser.

We're using both kinds of Cookies, Session and Persistent, for these reasons:

- **Necessary / Essential Cookies**
  - **Type**: Session Cookies
  - **Administered by**: Us
  - **Purpose**: These Cookies are super important for hooking you up with services available through the Website and letting you use some of its cool features. They help verify users and stop any shady, fraudulent use of user accounts. Without these Cookies, the services you're asking for can't be provided, and we're only using them to deliver those very services to you.

- **Cookies Policy / Notice Acceptance Cookies**
  - **Type**: Persistent Cookies
  - **Administered by**: Us
  - **Purpose**: These Cookies are all about figuring out if users have given their OK to use Cookies on the Website.

- **Functionality Cookies**
  - **Type**: Persistent Cookies
  - **Administered by**: Us
  - **Purpose**: These Cookies let us remember the choices you make when you're chilling on the Website, like recalling your login deets or language preferences. The whole point of these Cookies is to give you a more personalized experience and save you the hassle of typing in your preferences every single time you use the Website.

Wanna know more about the Cookies we're using and what choices you have? Just swing by our Cookies Policy or hit up the Cookies section of our Privacy Policy.
